Life at Covenant University

Covenant University is known for its discipline, spiritual atmosphere, and strong academic culture. From the early morning chapel services to late-night study sessions, the environment pushes you to develop yourself beyond academics.

The No-Light Hours Experience

The 8am–6pm no-light schedule during weekdays forces students to plan wisely. Most people stay in classrooms, use the library, or work around campus power points. It’s stressful sometimes—but it also teaches time management.

Community & Student Culture

Student communities like Google Developer Groups, school fellowships, and tech hubs make CU an exciting place. There’s always someone building something, reading something, or planning something.
